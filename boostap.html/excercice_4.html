Taj Mahel
State: Uttar Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Description:

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of eternal love.

Goa

State: Goa

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Description:

Famous for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and water sports. Popular beaches include Baga, Calangute, and Anjuna.

Manali

State:Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: April–June & Decembe

Description:

Manali is a scenic hill station in the Himalayas known for adventure sports. Snow-capped mountains and verdant valleys surround the town.

Varanasi

State:Uttar Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: October–March

Description:

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world and a spiritual hub. Pilgrims perform sacred rituals along the ghats of the Ganges River. Ancient temples and narrow lanes reflect centuries of history. The evening Ganga Aarti is a mesmerizing spiritual experience.

Udaipur

State:Rajasthan

Best Time to Visit: October–March

Description:

Udaipur is known as the City of Lakes, with romantic palaces and serene water views. The City Palace is an architectural marvel overlooking Lake Pichola. Traditional boat rides offer peaceful vistas of historic temples and ghats.

Shimla

State:Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit: March–June & December

Description:

Shimla is a hill station known for its colonial architecture and pleasant climate. The Mall Road offers shopping, cafes, and panoramic mountain views. Jakhu Temple and Ridge offer cultural and scenic attractions. Winter snowfall transforms it into a picturesque winter wonderland.
